Privacy Policy

Mind Defender — Gugus Factory

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Mind Defender is a free offline game developed by Gugus Factory. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data we collect

Mind Defender does not collect any personal information directly. The app stores your high score and settings locally on your device only. No data is sent to any server owned by Gugus Factory.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your device's Advertising ID to show relevant ads. This may include data such as device information and usage data. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Local storage

The app saves the following data locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences: your high score, last level reached, sound preference, and vibration preference. This data never leaves your device.

Google Play Games

This app integrates Google Play Games Services. If you sign in with your Google account, Google may collect your display name, profile photo, and game activity. This data is managed by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy. You can manage or delete your Google Play Games data at myaccount.google.com.

Children's privacy

Mind Defender is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
