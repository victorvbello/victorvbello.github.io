What is Mind Defender?

Mind Defender is a fast, hyper-casual arcade survival game for Android. Waves of enemies close in from every direction — your only job is to tap them before they reach your core. The longer you survive, the faster and harder they come.

Features

  • Five distinct enemy types, each with its own look and movement pattern
  • Skill system — Shield, Nuke, Freeze, and Regen to turn the tide mid-run
  • Mind Shards economy — earn currency through play, spend it in the in-game store
  • Fully playable offline — no internet connection required
  • Optional Google Play Games sign-in for leaderboards
  • No account required, no in-app purchases
